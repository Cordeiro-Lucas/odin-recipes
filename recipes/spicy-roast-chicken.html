Spicy Rapid Roast Chicken

Description

The Spicy Rapid Roast Chicken is a fast, simple, and delicious way to enjoy your chicken. Give it a simple seasoning, a small coating, and put it in the oven!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (3 Pounds recommended).
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano.
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil.
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika.
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper.

Step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Rinse chicken thoroughly inside and out under cold running water and remove all fat. Pat dry with paper towels.
  3. Put chicken into a small baking pan. Rub with olive oil. Mix the salt, pepper, oregano, basil, paprika and cayenne pepper together and sprinkle over chicken.
  4. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Lower the oven to 400°F (205°C) and continue roasting to a minimum internal temperature of 165°F (74°C), about 40 minutes more. Let cool 10 to 15 minutes and serve.
